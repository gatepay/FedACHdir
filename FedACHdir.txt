			<p>The Federal Reserve Banks' E-Payments Routing Directory provides basic routing information for Fedwire<sup>&reg;</sup> Funds Service,Fedwire Securities Service, and FedACH<sup>&reg;</sup> transactions. This information is synchronized with the Fedwire and FedACH databases daily and is provided free of charge solely as a service to financial institutions to help them process and settle transactions efficiently. The information contained in the directory may not be sold, relicensed, or otherwise used for commercial gain. You may link to this website, but you may not charge a fee for referring a person to this site or for providing a link to this site.</p>
			<p>If your browser supports HTTPS, you can download a plain text file by using the "File" and "Save As" options on your browser's toolbar. Once saved, this file may be imported into most standard spreadsheet or database management software for sorting or printing data. You may choose to receive all Fedwire participants, all FedACH participant RDFIs with commercial receipt volume, or just those entries for one group or the other that have been updated since a previously specified date. If you have questions regarding the Directory download, check out our <a href="/resources/routing-number-directory/faqs.html">Frequently Asked Questions</a>.</p>
